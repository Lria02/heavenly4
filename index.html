Super Mars 69th

as the descendant of the mighty Superman, Super Mars the 69th carries the weight of his royal bloodline, blessed with a unique and powerful trait—his Z+- blood type. He is a being of extraordinary strength and an unwavering commitment to helping those in need, with a special focus on single mothers who seek his help. His heart, pure and noble, has made him known far and wide as The Supermom Helper. His powers are as legendary as they are peculiar. With nothing more than a gaze, Super Mars can bestow life, his royal blood granting him the ability to impregnate women with a single look. Anyone who comes into contact with him is instantly overcome by an intense attraction—his mere presence ignites desires and passions, as his superhuman physique charms and enthralls all who encounter him. But despite his royal lineage and extraordinary powers, Super Mars has fallen on hard times. Stripped of wealth and forced to live in a humble state, he finds himself facing an unexpected dilemma—he needs a job Relying on his unique abilities, Super Mars decides to turn his talents to a new venture: Project Mars. This ambitious initiative is his way of giving back, creating a company that uses his remarkable gifts for the benefit of humanity. Through Project Mars, he aims to help those in need, elevate the status of single mothers, and, ultimately, restore his place in the world, one noble act at a time.
